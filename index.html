About

There are multiple characteristics of wine including sweetnes; acidity; and flavor, but what are the makings of a good wine? The two datasets we chose are related to red and white variants of the Portuguese "Vinho Verde" wine. Vinho Verde comes from a small region in Northern Portugal known for its super-value whites, reds, and ros√©s. These wines are loved for mouth-zapping acidity, subtle carbonation, and lower alcohol. For our project, we are going to explore how fixed acidity, volatile acidity, citric acid, residual sugar, chlorides, free sulfur dioxide, total sulfur dioxide, density, pH, sulphates, and alcohol vary among quality of wine score between 0 and 10.
